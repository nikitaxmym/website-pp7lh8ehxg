Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Giving

Operation CR Inc, is a tax-exempt (501c3), not-for-profit corporation organized and operated exclusively for charitable purposes. We believe that every child deserves access to proper nutrition and care.

Growing up in a Martial Arts and Christian based home, the concept of giving was always part of my traditions. We started out in our franchise by raising over 100K dollars for St. Judes Children's hospital and many other organizations.

Teaching our community that giving is also part of growth, we established Operation Child Rescue in efforts to give back to the world.

Message from Our Founder

Clinton Oh

As a seasoned entrepreneur and CEO of multiple franchise companies, I've been blessed with success in business. But true fulfillment comes from giving back to those who need it most.

"Teaching our community that giving is also part of growth - this philosophy drives everything we do at Operation Child Rescue."

Through my various business ventures including Next Level Media and the Be Aligned coaching program, I've learned that success isn't just measured by profit, but by the positive impact we make in the world.

Operation Child Rescue represents our commitment to ensuring no child goes hungry. Together, we can reach our goal of providing 1 million meals to children worldwide.
